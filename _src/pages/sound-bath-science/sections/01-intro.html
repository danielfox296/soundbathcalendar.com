What does the science actually say about sound baths?

Honestly: less than the marketing says, and more than nothing. Small studies suggest a sound bath can reduce self-reported tension, anxiety, and low mood in the short term — roughly what you would expect from an hour of guided deep rest. Claims that sound baths heal disease, repair cells, or rebalance the body's frequencies are not supported by evidence. If you go expecting a profoundly relaxing hour rather than a treatment, the science is on your side.

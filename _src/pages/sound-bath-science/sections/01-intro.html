What does the science actually say about sound baths?

A fair question, and it deserves a fair answer. The research so far is young but encouraging in a modest way: small studies find that people leave sessions reporting less tension, anxiety, and low mood — roughly what you would hope from an hour of guided deep rest. The larger hopes that travel with sound healing have not yet been borne out by research, and this page holds both truths at once: the practice gives something real, and the honest account of it is enough.

Where sound baths come from

The sound bath as you will find it on this calendar — a room of mats, an hour of gongs and bowls — is a young practice with old roots. It took its present shape in the West over the last century, drawing on gong traditions of East and Southeast Asia, India's contemplative practice of nada yoga, and the metal bowls of the Himalayan region — whose story is more modern, and more interesting, than the shorthand usually suggests. The traditions deserve honest credit, and the true story is the richer one.

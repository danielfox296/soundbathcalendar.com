Is a sound bath religious?

Mostly no — though the honest answer has more texture than a yes or a no. Most sound baths are not affiliated with any religious space, practitioner, or tradition: you lie down, instruments are played, and nothing is asked of your beliefs. At the same time, the practice carries aesthetics and conventions that sit outside mainstream Western music, and it is reasonable to notice that. This page holds the questions people actually bring — from practicing faith, from skepticism, from plain curiosity. The calendar does not sit in judgment of any practitioner's approach, and it will not sit in judgment of your hesitation either.

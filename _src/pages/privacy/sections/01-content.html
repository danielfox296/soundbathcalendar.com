Privacy

A calendar doesn't need much of you, so we don't take much. What we collect, what we do with it, and how to leave — all of it on one page.

What we collect

Two things, and nothing quietly on the side:

  • An email address — only if you sign up for the weekly digest. The form asks for your email and nothing else, and it's used for exactly one thing: sending you that email.
  • Basic analytics — the site uses Google Analytics 4 to see which pages get read and roughly where visitors come from, so we know which cities and sessions people actually care about.

The digest

One email a week, Thursday morning: the coming week's sound baths on the Front Range. Every email carries a one-click unsubscribe — use it any time and you're off the list right away.

What we never do

We don't sell your data, and we don't hand the digest list to organizers, venues, or anyone else. The list exists to send the digest; that's the whole job.

Who keeps this

Sound Bath Calendar is kept from Littleton, Colorado. Questions, corrections, or want your address taken off by hand? hello@soundbathcalendar.com — a person reads it.

432 Hz, explained honestly

432 Hz is an alternative tuning standard: the note A tuned to 432 vibrations per second instead of the modern convention of 440. Some musicians and facilitators prefer how it feels, and instruments tuned this way sound very slightly lower. There is no solid evidence that 432 Hz has unique healing or physiological effects — it is a tuning preference with devoted fans, not a proven therapy.

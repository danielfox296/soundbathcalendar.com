Singing bowls vs. gongs

Singing bowls produce steady, focused tones you can rest inside; gongs produce dense, evolving waves of overtones that build and recede. Bowls tend to feel gentler and more even, gongs more dramatic and physical. Most sound baths use both, and neither requires anything from you but lying down and listening.

Rooms worth knowing right now

The six rooms keeping the most sessions on the calendar this month — what's on at each, what it costs, and what's next.

By Sound Bath Calendar · July 22, 2026

The calendar tracks every sound bath we can find across the Front Range — 62 upcoming sessions as this goes up. This is a plain cut of that list: the rooms with the most on it right now, what they charge, and what's next at each.

No ranking beyond the count, and no reviews. Just where the sessions are, gathered in one place.

Singing Bowls of the Rockies · Colorado Springs

No room has more on the calendar right now: seven sessions between now and August 8, all at the studio on South Sierra Madre Street, nearly all in the $39–45 range. The formats rotate — reiki paired with sound, somatic breathwork, a vagus-nerve session, chakra work — while the room stays the same. Next up: Reiki & Sound Bath feat. Megan Odom on Friday, July 24.

Harmonist Sanctuary · Denver

Four sessions on the books at this Denver room, and no two alike: a gong journey, a creative-poetry sound bath, a candlelight bath with Cyndi Ortiz, and a First Friday "flow state" session in August. The listings don't publish prices, so check the ticket page. Next: Creative Poetry Sound Bath on Saturday, July 25.

The Denver Village · Denver

Three upcoming at The Denver Village, including a Buck Moon Sound Bath with Shelby Moor this Sunday, July 26, and a co-regulation sound bath on August 7.

Rocky Mountain Restore & Stretch · Fort Collins

Fort Collins' steadiest listing: this room runs a gong journey most Thursday evenings ($40), plus two dates outdoors — an Outdoor Full Moon Gong Journey on Wednesday, July 29 and a Sunday-morning session at Horsetooth Reservoir on August 2.

1545 S Pearl St · Denver

The room behind this South Pearl Street address doesn't list a studio name, but it keeps a steady 7 p.m. slot: Breathwork + Sound Healing Immersion on Monday, July 27, then a Full Moon Sound Medicine Journey on Friday, July 31.

The Next Us · Colorado Springs

Both upcoming sessions at The Next Us are community sound baths that cost little or nothing — one listed free, one donation-based. Next: Community Candle Lit Sound Bath on Friday, July 31. If cost is the barrier, start here.

Further out, worth a calendar note

  • A Solar Eclipse Sunset Gong Bath in North Boulder Park on Tuesday, August 11 — outdoors, timed to the eclipse.
  • Kundalini in the Park, same park, Sunday morning, August 2 — donation-based.

How this list was made

One measure only: how many upcoming sessions each room had on the calendar on July 22, 2026, when this was written. Dates, prices, and titles come straight from the listings, which change often — the calendar is always the current version of the truth, and the map shows where every room is.
